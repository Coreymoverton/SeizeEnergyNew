Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about SEIZE Energy? We've got answers! Find everything you need to know about our tropical energy drinks.

General Questions

What makes SEIZE Energy different from other energy drinks?

SEIZE Energy stands out with our unique tropical flavors sourced from real fruit extracts, zero sugar formula, and natural caffeine from green coffee beans. We focus on clean energy without artificial colors or excessive sweeteners, delivering sustained energy that won't crash.

How much caffeine is in SEIZE Energy?

Each 16 FL OZ can of SEIZE Energy contains 160mg of natural caffeine sourced from green coffee beans. This provides the perfect energy boost for most people without over-stimulation.

Is SEIZE Energy really zero sugar?

Yes! SEIZE Energy contains zero grams of sugar. We use natural fruit flavors and zero-calorie sweeteners to achieve our delicious tropical taste while keeping calories low at just 15 per can.

What flavors are currently available?

We currently offer two signature flavors: Pineapple Guava and Orange Dragonfruit. Both are crafted with real fruit extracts for authentic tropical taste. We're constantly working on new exciting flavors based on customer feedback!

Health & Ingredients

Are SEIZE Energy drinks safe to consume daily?

SEIZE Energy is made with natural ingredients and contains 160mg of caffeine per can. For most healthy adults, this amount is well within safe daily caffeine limits (up to 400mg). However, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider if you have any concerns about caffeine consumption.

Does SEIZE Energy contain artificial colors or preservatives?

No artificial colors! Our vibrant tropical colors come naturally from our fruit extracts. We use minimal preservatives, only what's necessary to maintain freshness and safety, following all FDA guidelines.

Is SEIZE Energy suitable for vegans?

Yes! SEIZE Energy is 100% vegan-friendly. All our ingredients are plant-based, including our natural caffeine from green coffee beans and all fruit extracts.

What vitamins and nutrients are in SEIZE Energy?

Each can contains essential B-vitamins including Niacin (B3), Vitamin B6, and Vitamin B12, which support energy metabolism. You'll also get natural vitamin C from our fruit extracts and antioxidants from dragonfruit and other tropical ingredients.

Purchasing & Availability

Where can I buy SEIZE Energy?

SEIZE Energy is available at major convenience stores, grocery stores, fitness centers, and online retailers. Use our store locator to find the nearest location, or order online for home delivery.

Do you offer bulk or subscription options?

Yes! We offer bulk purchasing options for gyms, offices, and events. We also have subscription services for regular delivery to your door with discounts for subscribers. Contact us for more details on bulk and subscription options.

What's the shelf life of SEIZE Energy?

SEIZE Energy has a shelf life of 12 months from the production date when stored in a cool, dry place. The best-by date is printed on the bottom of each can. For best taste, we recommend consuming within this timeframe.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within the United States and Canada. We're working on expanding to international markets. Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know when SEIZE Energy becomes available in your country!
