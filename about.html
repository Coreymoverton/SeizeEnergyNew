Our Story

From tropical dreams to energy reality - discover how SEIZE became the energy drink that fuels adventures worldwide.

Born in Paradise

SEIZE Energy was born from a simple idea: energy drinks didn't have to be boring or artificial. Our founder, Corey Overton, a passionate adventurer and tropical fruit enthusiast, noticed that most energy drinks relied on synthetic flavors and overwhelming sweetness.

After countless hours of research and development in tropical locations around the world, we discovered the perfect blend of natural ingredients that deliver sustained energy while transporting your taste buds to paradise.

From the beaches of Hawaii to the rainforests of Costa Rica, we sourced the finest tropical fruits and natural energy compounds to create something truly unique in the energy drink market.
